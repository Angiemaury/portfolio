ABOUT ME

Hey, I’m Angie Maury — a travel photographer, street art lover, and visual artist from Barranquilla, Colombia ,currently based in Europe.

Photography, for me, is about capturing the magic in the everyday — vibrant colors, raw moments, quiet streets,

and the little details that often go unseen.

I’m also passionate about documenting special events, from weddings to cultural gatherings,

and creating imagery for advertising and commercial projects that tells a story and connects with people.

I’m drawn to the beauty that lives outside the frame — the walls with stories, the energy of a city, the feeling of just being there.

This space is a reflection of how I see the world.

Every photo has a heartbeat, a mood, a reason it made me stop and click.

Take your time, explore the gallery, and if something sparks your interest — let’s connect.

I’d love to hear from you and discuss how we can create something memorable together.
